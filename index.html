Do you accept new patients and CDCP plans?

Yes! We are a premier dentist accepting new patients in Hamilton. We proudly accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP), along with most major private insurance providers. We believe in making quality dental care accessible to everyone in our community.

Can I get a same-day dental appointment?

Absolutely. We reserve daily schedule slots specifically for urgent cases. If you are experiencing pain or a dental emergency, call our office early in the morning to secure a same-day dental appointment. Walk-ins are also welcome for emergency dentistry in Hamilton.

Is there a dental clinic near me that offers weekend hours?

If you are searching for a "dental clinic near me" with flexible hours, Dentist Hamilton is your solution. We operate on Saturdays and offer after-hours emergency support, ensuring you never have to wait until Monday for urgent dental care.

What should I do in a dental emergency before arriving?

For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist in milk or saliva. For severe tooth pain, rinse with warm salt water and apply a cold compress to the outside of your cheek. Call us immediately so we can prepare for your arrival and provide rapid tooth pain relief.
